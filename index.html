Dr. Scott Jamison

Board-Certified Internal Medicine  ·  Functional Medicine Certified Practitioner-Master

Dr. Scott Jamison is a board-certified physician in Internal Medicine with nearly four decades of clinical experience dedicated to helping patients achieve lasting health through evidence-based, personalized care. After earning his medical degree from the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School, he completed his Internal Medicine residency at Baylor University Medical Center, where he developed a strong foundation in the diagnosis and treatment of complex adult medical conditions.

Throughout his 39-year career, Dr. Jamison has combined the principles of traditional Internal Medicine with the science of Functional Medicine to identify and address the root causes of chronic disease rather than simply treating symptoms. As a Functional Medicine Certified Practitioner-Master (FMCP-M), he integrates advanced diagnostics, lifestyle medicine, nutrition, targeted supplementation, and precision therapeutics to create individualized treatment plans for each patient.

His clinical focus includes preventive medicine, wellness optimization, hormone replacement therapy for men and women, age-management medicine, metabolic health, and longevity. Dr. Jamison is passionate about helping patients maintain vitality, preserve cognitive and physical function, optimize energy and performance, and reduce their long-term risk of chronic disease through proactive, evidence-based care.

His approach emphasizes listening carefully to each patient's story, uncovering the underlying drivers of illness, and developing comprehensive treatment strategies that restore health and improve quality of life.

Dr. Jamison believes that the future of medicine lies in preventing disease before it occurs while helping individuals optimize health at every stage of life. His mission is to partner with patients to maximize longevity, enhance performance, and support healthy aging through scientifically grounded, individualized Functional Medicine.
