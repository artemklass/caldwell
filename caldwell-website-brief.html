What We Are

Private medicine for men who perform.

Caldwell Private Health is a physician-supervised concierge health membership for high-income men. One flat monthly fee. No insurance. No waiting rooms. One dedicated clinician who knows you, manages your hormones, your labs, your training, and your nutrition — and is available when you need them.

Target client: male founders, executives, and professionals aged 35–60 earning $250K+. Based in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Copy Direction
Write like this
Direct. Short sentences. No fluff. "One clinician. Your protocol." not "We offer personalized care solutions." Performance language — optimize, precision, evolve. Address the man, not the condition.
Avoid
Wellness language. Spa aesthetics. Insurance-adjacent terms. "Patients" (use "members"). "Treatment" (use "protocol"). Anything that sounds like a GP office or a supplement brand.
